## Thermostats and Barostats: Ensembles in MD Simulations

Examples based on the official SIESTA [tutorial](https://docs.siesta-project.org/projects/siesta/en/stable/tutorials/basic/molecular-dynamics/) including:

- NVE: Isolated system (simple Verlet MD)
- NVT: Nose-Hoover Thermostat
- NpT: Nose-Hoover Thermostat and Rahman-Parrinello Barostat

Introduction to the microscopic interpretation of temperature and thermostats [here](https://saco.csic.es/s/tcn83zymYAAMCwK).
